My document about Dwarf Fortress

Dwarf fortress is a video game made in the c programing language that revolves around running a fortress of dwarves. The player does not control a living being directly but instead interacts with the settlement through things like orders which dwarves will eventually do upon finding time based on a number of variables in their simulated lives. This game is notable for its in depth and detailed simulations, for example every time you generate a world you generate 100 years of its history, empires rise and fall, heroes are forged and remembered, and thousands of lives are simulated all before you start playing the game.
